# References

The full annotated bibliography lives in
[`references/papers.md`](https://github.com/HaavardHvarnes/INLA.jl/blob/main/references/papers.md);
this page collects the load-bearing papers for users.

## Method papers

- **Rue, Martino & Chopin (2009)**, *Approximate Bayesian inference
  for latent Gaussian models by using integrated nested Laplace
  approximations*, JRSS-B. The original INLA paper. The simplified
  Laplace, CCD integration, and skew-correction recipes all come
  from here.
- **Rue & Held (2005)**, *Gaussian Markov Random Fields*. The book.
  GMRFs.jl is a partial Julia reimplementation of its numerical core.
- **Lindgren, Rue & Lindström (2011)**, *An explicit link between
  Gaussian fields and Gaussian Markov random fields: the SPDE
  approach*, JRSS-B. The basis of `INLASPDE.jl`.
- **Riebler et al. (2016)**, *An intuitive Bayesian spatial model for
  disease mapping that accounts for scaling*. The BYM2
  parameterisation we use.
- **Simpson, Rue, Riebler, Martins & Sørbye (2017)**, *Penalising
  model component complexity: a principled, practical approach to
  constructing priors*. PC priors throughout the ecosystem.
- **Fuglstad, Simpson, Lindgren & Rue (2019)**, *Constructing priors
  that penalize the complexity of Gaussian random fields*. PC-Matérn
  prior on (range, σ).
- **Freni-Sterrantino, Ventrucci & Rue (2018)**, *A note on
  intrinsic conditional autoregressive models for disconnected
  graphs*. The disconnected-graph sum-to-zero correction we apply
  to BYM, BYM2, and Besag.

## Validation datasets

Each ships as a `JLD2` fixture under
[`packages/LatentGaussianModels.jl/test/oracle/fixtures/`](https://github.com/HaavardHvarnes/INLA.jl/tree/main/packages/LatentGaussianModels.jl/test/oracle/fixtures)
or
[`packages/INLASPDE.jl/test/oracle/fixtures/`](https://github.com/HaavardHvarnes/INLA.jl/tree/main/packages/INLASPDE.jl/test/oracle/fixtures),
generated by R scripts pinned to specific R-INLA versions in
[`scripts/generate-fixtures/`](https://github.com/HaavardHvarnes/INLA.jl/tree/main/scripts/generate-fixtures).

| Dataset | Used for |
|---|---|
| Scotland lip cancer (`scotland_bym2`, `scotland_bym`) | BYM2 / BYM areal |
| Pennsylvania lung cancer (`pennsylvania_bym2`)        | BYM2 areal |
| Sardinia (synthetic `synthetic_disconnected_besag`)   | Disconnected ICAR |
| Meuse zinc (`meuse_spde`)                              | SPDE / geostatistics |
| Tokyo rainfall                                         | Cyclic RW2 + Bernoulli |
| Synthetic Gamma / NegBin / Generic / Seasonal / Leroux | Per-component oracles |
